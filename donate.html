Donate

Take Action: Join the fight to end the elephant crisis by taking the No-Ivory Pledge at The Elephant Crisis Fund.
Then, help to save elephants by donating to these organizations.

Africa

Wildlife Conservation Network
(USA)

The Wildlife Conservation Network combats the poaching crisis by supporting efforts worldwide that work to stop poaching, trafficking, and demand for ivory.

Save the Elephants
(Kenya)

Headquartered in Nairobi, Save the Elephants is a world-leading elephant research & conservation organisation devoted to securing a future for elephants.

The David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust
(Kenya)

Through its acclaimed Orphans' Project, The David Sheldrick Wildlife trust has successfully hand-raised over 150 infant elephants and has accomplished its long-term conservation priority by effectively reintegrating orphans back into wild herds.

Elephants for Africa
(Botswana / South Africa)

Founded by Dr. Kate Evans, Elephants for Africa conducts research to understand the ecological and social requirements of African elephants, and put these into the context of human-wildlife conflict.

Worldwide

International Elephant Foundation
(USA)

IEF's mission is to create a sustainable future for elephants by investing resources to support elephant conservation, education, research, and management programs worldwide.
